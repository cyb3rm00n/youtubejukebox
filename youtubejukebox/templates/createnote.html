How to Make a Request:


  1. Look your song up on YouTube, try to find a video that contains the official audio/lyrics to ensure that there are no extra things in the song
    • Sometimes, they like to get a little funky and add extra things into the music video that take away from the listening experience
  2. Copy the link to your song (you might need to click "share" on mobile)
  3. Paste the link in the form (above)
  4. Click "Request", the page will begin to load (this might take a few moments, just be patient)
  5. You will be brought back to the queue, you should see your song on there!
